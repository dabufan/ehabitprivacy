Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to EasyHabit! We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains in detail how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. Please read and understand the entire content of this policy carefully.

1. Information Collection

Types of information we collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Data: Habit creation, check-in records, app usage statistics
  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device
  • Optional Features: If you enable iCloud sync, data will be synced to your iCloud account

2. Information Usage

How we use your information:

  • Core Functionality: Habit creation, check-in records, data statistics
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to optimize user experience
  • Technical Support: Help resolve technical issues and user feedback
  • Personalized Experience: Provide relevant features based on your usage habits

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless:

  • You explicitly consent
  • Required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • For iCloud service synchronization (only when you enable it)

4. Data Security

Measures we take to protect your data:

  • Local encrypted storage
  • Secure iCloud synchronization (if enabled)
  • Regular security updates
  • Minimal data collection principle

5. In-App Purchase Information

About In-App Purchases:

  • Purchase information is processed by Apple, we cannot access your payment information
  • Purchase status is only used to unlock app features
  • Purchase records are stored on your device
  • You can restore purchases through Apple's "Restore Purchases" feature

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data (by deleting the app)
  • Stop using the app
  • Contact us for support
  • Choose whether to enable iCloud sync

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not specifically targeted at children under 13:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If you are a parent or guardian and find that your child has provided personal information, please contact us
  • We will immediately delete the relevant information

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy:

  • Updates will be published on this page
  • Major changes will be notified through in-app notifications
  • Continued use of the app indicates your agreement to the updated policy

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy:

  • Email: usehabit@gmail.com
  • We will respond to your inquiries within a reasonable time
  • We welcome your feedback and suggestions

Thank you for choosing EasyHabit. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing an excellent service experience.
